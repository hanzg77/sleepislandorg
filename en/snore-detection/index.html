FAQ

Is phone-based snore detection accurate?

Sleep Island uses AI algorithms to analyze audio and distinguish between snoring, sleep talking, and ambient noise. It's accurate enough for daily snoring awareness. The app also filters out music and conversation interference.

Does the screen need to stay on?

No. Snore detection runs in the background with the screen off. It won't interfere with charging or your morning alarm.

When should I see a doctor about snoring?

If detection shows severe snoring (more than 30 events per hour), see a doctor to check for obstructive sleep apnea (OSA). Your Sleep Island report can serve as useful reference data.
